The best movies according to Vicki

My top 3 movies of all time.

The Fountain

Tragically beautiful time-and-space-jumping spiritual and existential saga.

The Dancer in the Dark

Extremely heartbreaking story of a mother losing her sight and her child as a result of terrible circumstances. Bjork is a powerhouse of an actor.

Coraline

Drak and unsettling tale of a young girl who discovers a new dimension where everything is not as it seems. Burton-esque.
